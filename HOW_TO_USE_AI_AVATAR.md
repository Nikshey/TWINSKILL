# How to Use the AI Avatar Feature

This guide explains how to use the AI avatar feature that has been implemented in the Twinskill application.

## Prerequisites

1. A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
2. A registered account with a profile photo
3. Access to the Twinskill application

## Feature Overview

The AI avatar feature provides:
- Personalized AI teacher based on your profile photo
- Lip-synced responses to your questions
- Natural voice synthesis
- Interactive learning experience

## Step-by-Step Usage

### 1. Registration with Profile Photo

When registering for the first time:
1. Fill in your name, email, phone, and password
2. **Important**: Upload a clear profile photo
3. Click "Register" to create your account

### 2. Creating Your AI Avatar

After logging in:
1. Click on the menu icon (â˜°) in the top right corner
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Click the "Create AI Avatar" button
4. Wait for the avatar creation process to complete
5. Your avatar will be displayed in the profile section

### 3. Interacting with Your AI Avatar

To interact with your AI avatar:
1. Navigate to the "Learning" section by selecting a skill (Chess, Piano, or Yoga)
2. You'll see your AI avatar on the left side of the screen
3. Type your questions in the chat input at the bottom
4. Press Enter or click "Send" to submit your question
5. Your AI avatar will respond with both voice and lip-synced animation

### 4. Using Avatar Controls

You can control your avatar using the buttons:
- **Show Avatar**: Displays your avatar image
- **Hide Avatar**: Hides the avatar display
- **Stop Voice**: Stops any ongoing speech

## Technical Details

### Avatar Creation Process
1. Your profile photo is sent to the avatar creation service
2. An AI model generates a 3D avatar based on your facial features
3. The avatar is stored and linked to your profile
4. You can recreate your avatar anytime by clicking "Create AI Avatar"

### Voice and Lip-Sync Technology
1. When you ask a question, it's processed by our AI system
2. The response is converted to speech using text-to-speech technology
3. The avatar's mouth movements are synchronized with the speech
4. The result is displayed as a video of your avatar speaking

### Fallback Systems
If the advanced avatar system is unavailable:
- A static image of your avatar will be shown
- Standard browser text-to-speech will be used
- All functionality remains intact with slightly reduced visual experience

## Troubleshooting

### Avatar Not Appearing
1. Ensure you've uploaded a profile photo during registration
2. Click "Create AI Avatar" in your profile
3. Refresh the page after avatar creation

### Voice Not Working
1. Check that your browser supports text-to-speech
2. Ensure your device has audio capabilities
3. Check browser permissions for audio playback

### Avatar Not Responding
1. Make sure you're typing questions in the chat input
2. Press Enter or click "Send" to submit questions
3. Check your internet connection

## Best Practices
